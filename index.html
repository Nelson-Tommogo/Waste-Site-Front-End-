About chemba SmartWaste


Chemba SmartWaste combines IoT technology with web-based platforms to revolutionize waste management. With remote user control, it offers efficient monitoring and management of waste systems, promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility.

About chemba IoT


Chemba IoT is poised to address the pressing waste management challenges facing Nairobi, Kenya. By harnessing IoT technology, Chemba SmartWaste offers a comprehensive solution to efficiently monitor and manage waste systems across the city. Through remote control capabilities and real-time data insights, Chemba SmartWaste enables authorities to optimize waste collection routes, minimize environmental impact, and promote a cleaner, healthier urban environment for all residents.
